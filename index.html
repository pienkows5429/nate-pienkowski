Approach

How I help you accomplish this.

This work is about embedding lasting capability in your team, so the quality of what you produce holds up over time and at scale.

01

Equipping non-trainers to deliver training that holds up.

When the people delivering — or diagnosing, or running the whole cycle — aren't L&D specialists (managers, champions, super-users), they need structure that carries the expertise for them. I build that structure.

02

Strengthening your L&D team's capability.

When the goal is improving what your build team produces — quality, durability, outcomes that hold up — I work with them on methodology, frameworks, and the decisions that separate training that works from training that just gets shipped.

03

Bringing AI into L&D with rigor.

When you want AI in your function but the path isn't obvious, I help integrate it — into the methodology, into the team's workflow, into what they're capable of producing — without the magical thinking that's everywhere right now.
